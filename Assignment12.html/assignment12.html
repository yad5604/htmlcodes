To describe someones height, you can say that they are tall or short. Tall people are higher than short people.Someone who is thin and tall can be called lanky.To say someone is tall and also small,you can say they are petite.If you're talking about a child, they might be pint-sized.

Someone who weighs more than average can be curvy,well-built,full-bodied or heavy.(Curvy is usually used to describe women.) You might also say someone have some meat on their bones. This is a casual way of saying they are oveweight,and it might seem rude to someone sensitive.The opposite of overweight could be thin,slim or skinny

Light, yellowish hair can be described as blonde.But you can also call a person who has that kind of hair a blonde.(This means that blonde can be used as a noun or an adjective).A person with dark hair can be called a brunette.Someone with red hair can be called a redhead.

Besides what color of someone's hair is, you also can say they are long-haired or short-haired. You can say they have curly,straight or wavy hair.Sometimes hair can also be frizzy,which is when it looks puffy,with small,tight curls.If someone has no hair at all on their head,they are bald.

If someone dresses well, they are smartly dressed or they look smart.You can alsojust say they are well-dressed.If someone dresses fationably,they are stylish or trendy.If someone has bad taste in clothes,you can say they are unfationable.A less nice way to say this is frumpy.

Many people have mannerisms that appear when they are feeling negative(bad) emotions.If someone feels nervous or impatient,for example they might tap their fingers,crack their knuckles,bite their fingernails or chew the tip of their pencils.
